Jeremy Swayman

Welcome to my information page!

Hockey player for the University of Maine as well as a total outdoorsman

I have grown up living in the most majestic place on earth and learned to fall in love with nature at a young age. Anytime I was not in the rink, I was in the mountains, fishing on the river, or camping in the middle of no where. I have lived in Alaska, Colorado, South Dakota, and now Maine. All of these destinations were chosen by my hockey path, but I learned to fall in love with each place on a personal level and I feel fortunate to have partaken in the adventure I have this far!
